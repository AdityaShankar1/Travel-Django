About Aditya Shankar

Lead Developer & Travel Enthusiast

Why I Built This

I've always had a deep passion for exploring the diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage of India. Tamil Nadu, with its ancient temples, misty hills, and serene coastlines, has always been close to my heart. I built this Travel-Django project to simplify the way travelers plan their circuits across this beautiful state, combining modern technology with the spirit of adventure.

How to Use

It's simple! browse our Travel Packages on the home page, select a circuit that interests you, and customize your booking. You can также explore our Blogs section for detailed insights into various cities and landmarks. Once you've made a booking, you can track it in My Bookings and proceed with payment to confirm your spot.
